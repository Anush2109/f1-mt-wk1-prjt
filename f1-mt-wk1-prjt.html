City Description

The city is an incredible mixture of traditions and modernity.Hyderabad is known for its rich history, culture, cuisine, and architecture representing its unique character as a meeting point for North and South India, both geographically and culturally. Hyderabad has become the most well-liked conference venue in India also as in Asia, with several conferences and conferences going down within the city.Founded by Sulthan Mohammed Quli Qutub Shah the 5th Nizam of the Qutub Shahi dynasty in 1590 A.D.
